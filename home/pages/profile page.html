At FM Veterinary Drug Shop, we have worked on different projects and our aim is to improve the quality of, access to, and effective treatment practices.

Some of the projects are;

Organising a National Vaccination Day (we give out flyers to our clients informing them about the free vaccination that is going to take place)

Giving out loan and credit services for farmers, allowing them to procure essential drugs, vaccines, feed and pay at a later date.

Introducing modern farming Techniques for example, airtight containers (metal cans) to keep out rodents, pests and moisture.

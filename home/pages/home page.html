CEO Mirembe Sarah

I'm Mirembe Sarah.
The passionate CEO and Founder of FM Veterinary Drug Shop in Bukerere.
With five years of dedicated experience in this industry,
My mission is to empower local livestock farmers and pet owners by providing trusted, high-quality veterinary medicine that improves livestock productivity in the community.

We develop cutting-edge vaccines for both Poultry and Animals such as Kukustar. Beyond vaccines, we are committed to creating parasiticides to prevent and treat diseases in pets, poultry, and livestock.
